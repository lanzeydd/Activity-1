BS in Industrial Technology

The Bachelor of Science in Industrial Technology Program is a four-year degree program which prepares workers for supervisory and managerial positions in industries, or as shop proprietors or managers in civil and drafting technology, computer technology, electrical technology, food processing and service management, and garments and fashion design.

Specifically, the program aims to produce graduates who are able to:

bv
  • Practice the profession ethically and responsibly.
  • Keep abreast with the developments in technology.
  • Use techniques, skills, and modern technological tools necessary for professional practice.
  • Act as a member or team leader to manage projects in multidisciplinary environments by applying management principles.
  • Communicate effectively to the society the various technological projects and the benefits that can be derived from it.
  • Know contemporary issues and its implications to society.
  • Pursue lifelong learning for personal and professional growth through varied experiential and field-based opportunities.
  • Develop a web site employing full stack development.
